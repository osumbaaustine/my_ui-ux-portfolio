Client - Pace Africa

Pace is a sound gadget manufacturer in Nairobi, Kenya selling sound and entertainment devices to an urban market

Project Scope - The project was timed for two weeks with the user interfaces for a mobile e-commerce app. Pace Africa wanted to craft and develop the user experiences and user interfaces for their products selling pllatform. The UI/UX design was to show the initial processes of loging in and signing up to how the user browses products and buying the same.

Design Rationale

A seamless and simple but comprehensive design on an iOS platform.
